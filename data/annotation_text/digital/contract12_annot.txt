COLUMBIA STRATEGIC INCOME FUND
MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT dated as of November 1, 2003, between COLUMBIA FUNDS TRUST I, a
Massachusetts business trust ("Trust"), with respect to COLUMBIA STRATEGIC
INCOME FUND ("Fund"), and COLUMBIA MANAGEMENT ADVISORS, INC., an Oregon
corporation ("Advisor").
The Fund shall pay the Advisor monthly a fee at the annual rate of 0.65% of
the first $1 billion of the average daily net assets of the Fund, 0.60% on
the next $1 billion and 0.55% in excess of $2 billion.
